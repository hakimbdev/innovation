About Us

Girl Child Education Watch International is a dedicated non-governmental organization passionately committed to addressing the critical issue of out-of-school children, with a primary emphasis on empowering and educating girls affected by poverty. In a world where the intersection of gender and poverty often denies countless girls their right to education, our organization stands as a beacon of hope, working tirelessly to break down barriers and create pathways to learning for the most vulnerable.
